REALME 5 PRO

About Mobile:

Realme 5 Pro smartphone was launched on 20th August 2019. The phone comes with a 6.30-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1080x2340 pixels at a pixel density of 409 pixels per inch (ppi) and an aspect ratio of 19.5:9. Realme 5 Pro is powered by an octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 712 processor. It comes with 4GB of RAM. The Realme 5 Pro runs Android 9 Pie and is powered by a 4035mAh non-removable battery. The Realme 5 Pro supports VOOC fast charging.Learn more.
